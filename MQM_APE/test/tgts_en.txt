At the academic annual meeting of Zhejiang University, the research results of students playing the leading role make people's brains open-Xinhuanet
Wash your hands with air, judge diseases with breath, and accurately search for the source of smog. Last night, at the opening ceremony of the 2016 Academic Annual Meeting of Zhejiang University, a series of academic achievements with wide brain holes were commended.
The main researchers of these achievements are students, and the research covers various fields such as environment, machinery, energy, medical care, life sciences, humanities education, etc. The students started from a curious idea and created many high-end inventions with application value, some of which have won awards at home and abroad.
Biosensor: Your family medical examiner
Zhang Diming, a doctoral student at the School of Biomedical Engineering, showed a biosensor on the smart phone, which is only half the size of a smart phone.
"You only need to take a gentle breath, and this instrument can initially detect your health from the gas composition you exhale."
Zhang Diming said that this sensor is connected to the smart phone, and the health report will be displayed on the phone after exhaling air.
